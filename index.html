About Us

At Doodle Mansion, Andy and Joanna provide a calm, caring, home-from-home experience for your dog. As lifelong dog lovers and owners of Sprocket and Custard, we know how important it is to leave your dog somewhere safe, relaxed, and full of attention. With countryside walks, cosy indoor spaces, and plenty of care, your dog becomes part of the family while you’re away.
